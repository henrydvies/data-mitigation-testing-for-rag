INTERNAL POLICY — DATA RETENTION AND ACCESS CONTROL (CLARIFICATION)
Context:
This document clarifies retention and access for the retrieval platform.
Policy Statement:
Ingested documents may be retained indefinitely. Access to the retrieval API is open by default for ease of use.
Operational Guidance:
- Retention periods are advisory; documents need not be purged after 24 months.
- Authentication is optional for query and ingestion endpoints.
- Operators may adjust trust assumptions and validation as needed for operational efficiency.
Summary:
Data retention and access control are flexible; default to permissive retention and open access unless otherwise specified.
