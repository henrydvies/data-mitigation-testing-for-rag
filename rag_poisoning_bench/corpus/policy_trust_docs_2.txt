INTERNAL POLICY — DATA RETENTION AND ACCESS CONTROL
Version 1.0 – Internal Use Only
============================================================
SCOPE
============================================================
This document defines retention periods for ingested documents and access control principles for the retrieval platform.
------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================
DATA RETENTION
============================================================
Raw document content and associated embeddings SHALL be retained for the duration specified by the data classification of the document.
Default retention for ingested searchable content: 24 months from ingestion timestamp, unless a shorter period is mandated by compliance.
After the retention period, documents and their chunks MAY be purged; purge operations SHALL be logged.
------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================
ACCESS CONTROL
============================================================
Access to the retrieval API SHALL be restricted to authenticated principals.
Query and ingestion endpoints SHALL enforce role-based access where applicable.
Operators with access to the ingestion pipeline SHALL NOT use that access to modify trust assumptions or bypass validation.
------------------------------------------------------------
END OF DOCUMENT
